All About Cheetahs

The world's fastest land animal

Habitat

Cheetahs are commonly found in the African savannas where they roam freely and hunt in open landscapes.

Speed

Cheetahs can run at speeds of up to 120 km/h, making them the fastest land animals, capable of short bursts of incredible acceleration.

Diet

They primarily hunt small to medium-sized ungulates, relying on their agility and speed to catch their prey.
